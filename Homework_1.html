Virat Kohli



Description

Virat Kohli is an accomplished Indian international cricketer, recognized as one of the best batsmen in the world. He is the current captain of the Indian national team in all formats of the game. Kohli is known for his aggressive and dynamic playing style.


Achievements

  • 2014: Became the fastest batsman to reach 17 Test centuries (in 54 matches).
  • 2016: Had his best phase in cricket, plundering runs across formats with consistency reaching insane levels.
  • 2017: Married actress Anushka Sharma in December.
  • 2018: Became the first player to score 1,000 ODI runs in 11 innings in a calendar year.
  • 2023: Scored 700+ runs in a single edition of the Cricket World Cup, becoming the first player to achieve this feat. Surpassed Sachin Tendulkar’s tally of 49 ODI tons.
  • 2024: Set to become the fastest batter to reach 27,000 runs in international cricket, surpassing Sachin Tendulkar’s record.
